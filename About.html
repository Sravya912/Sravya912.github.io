About Us

We aim to explore and bring attention to prominent as well as lesser-known temples in Andhra Pradesh. We include their history, impressive architecture, and the cultural stories behind them. Our goal is to promote understanding of the local culture and pilgrimage while encouraging the interested.

Objectives

  • To make the exploration of temples in the state easy
  • Bringing out the uniqueness of unknown temples
  • Providing the stories and historical background of well-known temples
